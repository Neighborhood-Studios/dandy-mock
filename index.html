Questions

Fair asks.

How do Dandy visits work?＋
Your membership includes an average of 1.5 hours of handyman work each month. Use that time for shorter visits when something needs attention, or save it for a fully prepared half-day visit. Most members choose the half-day, but you can use your time however your home needs it.
What is a Dandy drop?＋
We bring Dandy to Atlanta one neighborhood at a time. First, we hire an exceptional handyman and dedicate him to that neighborhood. Then all 50 memberships become available on the same day. Once those spots are claimed, the neighborhood closes and we move on to the next.
Is it really the same handyman every time?＋
Yes. Your Dandyman is dedicated to just 50 members in one neighborhood, so you see the same familiar person visit after visit. Over time, he gets to know your home, your preferences, and what may need attention next.
What kinds of work can my Dandyman handle?＋
Your Dandyman can take care of common home repairs, routine maintenance, installations, adjustments, and lingering small projects. Think sticking doors, faulty fixtures, drywall patches, caulking, filter changes, furniture assembly, and the everyday work that keeps a home in good shape. View the list of services we provide here.
What routine maintenance does Dandy keep track of?＋
Dandy helps keep recurring home-care tasks on the radar, including things like HVAC filters, smoke detectors, water-heater flushing, and refrigerator-coil cleaning. We fold them into your visits so they do not become one more thing for you to remember.
How does Dandy prepare for a visit?＋
Before a visit, the Dandy team reviews your list, identifies what may be needed, and helps source the appropriate parts and supplies. That way, your Dandyman can arrive prepared and spend more of the visit getting things done.
What if a project is too large or specialized?＋
We’ll let you know before the visit. Your Dandyman can often handle part of the work, help diagnose the issue, or guide you toward the right licensed specialist when a job falls outside Dandy’s scope.
Can I cancel my membership?＋
Yes. Dandy is month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime.
