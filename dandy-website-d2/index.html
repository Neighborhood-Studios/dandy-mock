Questions

Fair asks.

How do the visits work?＋
Every month includes 1.5 hours of handyman work. Take it as a monthly short visit, or as a fully prepared half-day each quarter — most members choose the half-day, and you can switch cadences anytime in the app.
Is it really the same handyman every time?＋
Yes — that's the whole product. Your Dandyman is a full-time, background-checked Dandy employee assigned to about 50 homes in your neighborhood, including yours. If he's ever out sick, we'll tell you before the visit, never surprise you at the door.
What counts as autopilot?＋
Routine care every home needs: HVAC filter swaps, smoke detector batteries, first-story gutter checks, weatherstripping and caulk inspection, dryer vent clearing, and seasonal checks. Your Dandyman folds them into visits automatically.
What if a job is too big for a visit?＋
Your Dandyman will tell you straight, help you scope it, and connect you with a vetted specialist. We'd rather protect your trust than stretch a job.
Can I cancel?＋
Anytime, month-to-month. Fair warning: your neighborhood spot goes back into the drop, and drops sell out.
