Why we have a scope

A clear scope is how
every visit actually delivers.

— one

One dedicated generalist

Your Dandyman is a skilled, full-time generalist — the person you'd trust with the everyday work of a home. Staying in that lane is what keeps him excellent at it.

— two

Honest about timelines

Most tasks are finished within the visit itself. Some jobs need a part on order or a second pass — when that happens, your Dandyman tells you upfront, and it goes to the top of the list for the next visit.

— three

Licensed trades stay licensed

Roofs, panels, and foundations belong to specialty contractors — legally and practically. When your home needs one, we help you find the right professional.

Where we draw the line

What we don't do —
and why that's good for you.

To keep every visit efficient and safe, we don't perform:

  • ✕Roofing
  • ✕Major plumbing
  • ✕Major electrical
  • ✕HVAC repairs
  • ✕Tree work
  • ✕Foundation work
  • ✕Remodels
  • ✕Structural framing
  • ✕Hazardous material removal
  • ✕Work requiring specialty contractor licensing
✦ When a project falls outside our scope, we'll help you find the right professional. Your Dandyman will tell you straight, help you scope it, and connect you with a vetted specialist.
